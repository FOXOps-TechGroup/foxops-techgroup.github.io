关于 About

关于 FOXOps TechGroup。 About FOXOps TechGroup.

我们是谁

我们是 FOXOps TechGroup，其中 FOX 是 Foundation for O&M on XCPC 的简写。我们是一支专注于程序设计竞赛技术支持的学生团队，成员来自江西省多所高校。

我们的使命

让每一场竞赛都能稳定、高效地运行。我们希望通过专业的技术运维，为参赛选手、教练和赛事组织方提供可靠的服务保障。

我们在做什么

  • 竞赛系统运维：负责 DOMjudge、CMS 等竞赛平台的部署、配置与现场维护。
  • 赛事信息发布：收集、整理并发布赛事公告、日程、手册等关键信息。
  • 现场技术支持：在比赛现场提供网络、打印、评测系统等方面的技术保障。
  • 页面与工具开发：维护赛事信息网站，开发辅助工具提升运维效率。

我们的足迹

我们目前主要服务于江西省内的 XCPC 赛事，并逐步参与省外赛事的技术支持工作。

Who we are

We are FOXOps TechGroup, and FOX stands for Foundation for O&M on XCPC. We are a student team focused on technical support for competitive programming contests, with members from multiple universities in Jiangxi Province.

Our mission

We aim to make every contest run stably and efficiently. Through professional technical operations, we provide reliable services for contestants, coaches, and contest organizers.

What we do

  • Contest system operations: Deploy, configure, and maintain contest platforms such as DOMjudge and CMS on site.
  • Contest information publishing: Collect, organize, and publish key contest updates, schedules, and handbooks.
  • Onsite technical support: Provide network, printing, and judging system support during live contests.
  • Web & tooling development: Maintain the contest information website and build internal tools to improve operational efficiency.

Our footprint

We currently serve XCPC contests primarily in Jiangxi Province and are gradually expanding our technical support to contests outside the province.
